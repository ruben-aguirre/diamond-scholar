## The 8 Frames

1. **Stance** — bat cocked over right shoulder, face visible, calm ready pose
2. **Load** — weight shifts back, hands draw back, bat tips more vertical
3. **Stride** — front foot plants, hips open, hands/bat STILL back (separation)
4. **Initiation** — hands drop forward, bat falls to ~45°, body ~45% rotated
5. **Contact** — bat horizontal extended toward pitcher, back foot on toe (money frame)
6. **Extension** — arms past lead shoulder, "9" coming into view
7. **Wrap** — bat wrapping toward left shoulder, full back visible
8. **Finish** — bat fully wrapped, hands high, full back with "9" centered

**Critical:** feet stay anchored at the same pixel position across all 8 frames — only the body rotates and limbs move. No drift.
